Procedures

Here, you can find information on many different procedures that are performed by dentists.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a procedure that removes stains from the surface of your teeth. It is usually done to improve the appearance of your teeth.

Root Canal

A root canal is a procedure that removes the pulp from the inside of a tooth. It is usually done to treat a tooth that is infected or damaged.

Polishing

Polishing is a procedure that removes stains and plaque from the surface of your teeth. It is usually done after a dental cleaning.
